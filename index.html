Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 6, 2026

Rashiq is an offline fitness, calorie tracking, workout, and health habit tracking app.

1. Data Collection

Rashiq does not collect, upload, sell, or share personal data with external servers. The app does not require account registration or login.

2. Local Data Storage

Information entered by the user, such as meals, calories, workouts, weight records, fasting records, and app preferences, is stored locally on the user’s device.

3. Health and Fitness Data

Any nutrition, calorie, weight, or workout information entered in the app is used only to provide tracking features inside the app. This information remains on the device unless the user chooses to export or share it manually.

4. Internet and Third Parties

The offline version of Rashiq does not send user data to third-party analytics, advertising, or cloud storage services.

5. Children’s Privacy

Rashiq is not intended for children under the age of 13.

6. Data Deletion

Users can delete their local data by clearing the app data from device settings or uninstalling the app. If the app includes an in-app reset option, users may also delete their records from inside the app.

Delete Account

Delete Account / Delete Data

Rashiq does not require users to create an account. Therefore, there is no online account stored on our servers.

To delete your app data:

  1. Open your phone settings.
  2. Go to Apps.
  3. Select Rashiq.
  4. Choose Storage.
  5. Tap Clear Data, or uninstall the app.

This removes locally stored app data from your device. If you need help, contact: m.ahmed743@gmail.com
